Welcome to my personal website. I am a researcher interested in the advancing algorithms to make artificial agents more intelligent. I am currently pursuing my PhD in Robot Learning under Prof Gerhard Neumann at the ALR Lab, Karlsruhe Institute Of Technology. Before that I was a Data Scientist at the cybersecurity firm McAfee (Intel Security). Previously I worked with Intel for 2 years. I hold a post graduate degree in Machine Learning and Computing from the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology.

At present for my PhD, I focus on building probablisitc recurrent state space models and learning world models with it. In general I am interested in developing algorithms that can learn continually in a non-stationary environment and can solve long horizon tasks. I use neural network architectures that are backed by probalisitic principles to build these models.
